List of pesticides

  1. Malathion – Used to control a wide range of insects on crops and vegetables.
  2. Cypermethrin – Effective against pests like caterpillars and beetles.
  3. Imidacloprid – Commonly used for controlling sucking pests like aphids and whiteflies.
  4. Chlorpyrifos – Used for controlling termites and soil-dwelling insects
  5. Deltamethrin – A broad-spectrum pesticide for both agricultural and household pests.
  6. Carbaryl – Used to control beetles, caterpillars, and other pests on fruit and vegetables.
  7. Copper Sulfate – An organic fungicide and pesticide effective against fungal infections.
  8. Neem Oil – A natural pesticide used to control insects and fungal diseases.
  9. Sulfur Dust – Effective against mites, thrips, and powdery mildew
  10. Bacillus Thuringiensis (BT) – A biological pesticide used against caterpillars and moth larvae.
